Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book a ticket?

You can book tickets online through our website or mobile app. Simply select your route, choose your seats, and complete the payment process.

What is your cancellation policy?

Tickets can be cancelled up to 24 hours before departure for a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours of departure are subject to a 50% fee.

How early should I arrive before departure?

We recommend arriving at least 30 minutes before your scheduled departure time to ensure a smooth check-in process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, MTN Mobile Money, and Orange Money.
