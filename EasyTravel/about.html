About EasyTravel

Your trusted travel partner in Cameroon since 2020

Our Story

EasyTravel was founded with a simple mission: to make bus travel in Cameroon comfortable, reliable, and truly easy. What started as a small operation has grown into one of the country's most trusted transportation services.

We understand that every journey matters, whether it's a business trip, family visit, or adventure exploration. That's why we've built our service around the needs of our passengers, focusing on safety, comfort, and convenience.
