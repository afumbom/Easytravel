About EasyTravel

Your trusted partner in comfortable and reliable bus travel across Cameroon.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, EasyTravel was born from a simple idea: to make bus travel in Cameroon more accessible, comfortable, and enjoyable for everyone.

What started as a small operation with just two buses has grown into one of Cameroon's most trusted transportation networks, serving thousands of passengers daily across multiple cities.

Our commitment to safety, comfort, and customer satisfaction has earned us the trust of our passengers and established us as a leader in the transportation industry.

Our Values

Safety First

Your safety is our top priority. We maintain our vehicles to the highest standards and employ experienced drivers.

Customer Focus

We put our customers at the heart of everything we do, ensuring a pleasant travel experience from booking to arrival.

Reliability

We understand the importance of punctuality and strive to maintain consistent and reliable service.
