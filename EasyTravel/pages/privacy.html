Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 4, 2025

1. Introduction

At EasyTravel, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our bus ticket booking platform.

2. Information We Collect

Type of Data Purpose
Name and contact details To process your booking and send confirmations
Payment information To process ticket purchases securely
Travel details To book your tickets with bus agencies
Booking history To provide customer support and improve our service

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process your ticket bookings with bus agencies
  • Send booking confirmations and updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our booking platform
  • Comply with legal requirements

4. Information Sharing

We share your information with:

  • Bus agencies (only details needed for your booking)
  • Payment processors (for secure transactions)
  • Legal authorities (when required by law)

We never sell your personal information.

5. Data Security

We protect your data through:

  • Secure payment processing
  • Regular security updates
  • Controlled access to personal information
  • Encrypted data storage

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

7. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your booking experience and analyze site traffic. You can control cookie settings in your browser.
