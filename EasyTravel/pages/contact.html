Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book a ticket?
You can book tickets easily through our website. Simply use the search form on our homepage, select your route and preferred schedule, and follow the booking process. You can also visit our physical offices or call our booking hotline.
What is your cancellation policy?
Tickets can be cancelled up to 24 hours before departure for a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours of departure will be subject to a 50% cancellation fee. Please contact our customer service for assistance with cancellations.
How much luggage can I bring?
Each passenger is allowed one piece of luggage (up to 20kg) and one carry-on bag. Additional luggage can be arranged for an extra fee. Please contact us in advance for oversized items or special requirements.
